FAQ

How can I reserve/release a device?

  • Just click the radio button under the device you need, if it's not reserved by others.

  • If it's reserved, you can find the user who reserved it and can send a request to release.

How can I add a device?

  • Click on the add device details option in your menu and fill in the form to add new device details and click submit.

How to find device details?

  • Find the device you are looking for in device list and click on the device name or image tile to navigate to device details page.
